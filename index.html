The method

A plan that can survive its own execution.

Stable / 01

The goal stays above the plan.

Track revises tasks and assignments without quietly replacing the outcome they serve.

Visible / 02

Dependencies decide the route.

Independent work runs in parallel. Shared gates stay locked until their evidence arrives.

Adaptable / 03

Sessions are resources, not structure.

Split, merge, pause, or replace a session while the workstream and its handoff remain legible.

The scope gate

New work must earn its place.

Every new task, session, or expansion is checked against the current goal and observed need. Track keeps ambition visible without turning every idea into a commitment.

  • PassProceed at the smallest useful scope.
  • ReduceShrink the work, then check it again.
  • HoldKeep it visible, but do not execute it now.
